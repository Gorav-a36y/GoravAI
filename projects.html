Sparky Automation – Electrician AI Dispatcher

An AI agent that manages electrician jobs, pricing, scheduling, and client communication automatically.

Sparky acts as a virtual manager. It distinguishes between Simple Work (fan repair, bulb change) and Complex Work (rewiring, installations). It instantly quotes prices for simple jobs and collects detailed requirements (images, descriptions) for complex ones, generating official DOC files for the electrician.
